objet

2013 -

Blending electronic and rock influence, objet musical activities are centered around guitar playing, live looping and weird home-built sequencers.

(Re-)discovering old and new technology, playing drums, pushing keys and buttons, hiding some of my favorite musicians into my own creation.

About IN C (.composed by terry riley. one performer. one guitar. six amps. synchronized live loopers. one take. no overdub.) :

"A very interesting and worthwhile solo live performance of In C done with loopers. Any performance of the piece where the fragments are looped loses some of the excitement of an ensemble performance, but for one person to do this in one take is remarkable. It works." - William Denton / Bandcamp
